Thiviyan Thanapalasingam

Raised in Bochum, Germany, currently residing in Milton Keynes, UK. Thiviyan is an Assistant Researcher within the Intelligent Systems and Data Science (ISDS) Group at the Knowledge Media Institute (KMi).

He graduated from The University of Leicester with a 1st class Master's Thesis in Theoretical and Physical Chemistry. At KMi, he is working on solving meta-research problems for enhancing scientific communications and also supports the editorial process at Springer Nature. Thiviyan's research focuses on developing knowledge discovery systems.

In his spare time, he enjoys road cycling, running and likes to cram in more learnings at Coursera.
